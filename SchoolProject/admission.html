Admission Requirements

1. ENGLISH LANGUAGE REQUIREMENTS FOR ADMISSION

For the admission requirements for an individual program, please find your program of interest and scroll to the Admissions Requirements section on the program page. All international programs have English language requirements. Applicants for whom English is a second language must submit evidence of their ability in the English language as part of the application procedure.

The following chart below shows the score you need to achieve for the language test. The scores may be different based on the program credential. Our health career programs require a higher score because these careers involve interacting with patients. Please read the exceptions to find any programs that require specific scores.

2. TUITION AND FEES

Application Deposit

A $100 deposit must be received by DreamScape College when an application is submitted. This deposit is applied to tuition when the student begins classes.

Tuition Fees

International tuition fees vary between programs and certifications. International tuition fees for each program can be found within the program page under “Tuition Summary.”

Expected Living Expenses

In addition to the above fees, you will need an additional $1500 to $2000 (Canadian funds) per month to cover:

1. Accommodation
2. Transportation
3. Food
4. Books
5. School supplies
6. Entertainment
