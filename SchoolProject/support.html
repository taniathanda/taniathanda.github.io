HEALTH AND WELLNESS

Student Wellness Centre


Students at the London Campus will enjoy state-of-the-art facilities at the Student Wellness Centre (SWC). The SWC is home to a number of services to support students' physical and mental wellbeing.

Health Service


The Student Wellness Centre houses Fowler Kennedy Clinic and a pharmacy for students to have easy access to a range of medical services. Find additional information on your health plan.

Counselling Services


Our professionally trained counsellors have a wealth of experience and knowledge and can assist you in areas such as mental health, substance use, relationships, and other personal concerns that might impact your academic success.

Sexual Violence & Prevention


A Sexual Violence Prevention Advisor can provide support and information about your options, which may include: medical, counselling, reporting and academic accommodations.
